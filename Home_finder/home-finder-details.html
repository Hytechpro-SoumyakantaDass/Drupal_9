Description

Two-bedroom second floor flat in the popular Park Farm area of Port Glasgow. Property comprises livingroom, two double bedrooms, kitchen with access to a balcony and bathroom. Communal drying area to the rear of the building. The area is well served by a regular and frequent bus service. A range of shops are nearby along with primary and secondary schooling. There is approximately a total of thirty (30) stairs to the front door of this flat. Open to all Applicants in the General Needs category requiring two bedrooms and with a Stair Level coding of 15+
